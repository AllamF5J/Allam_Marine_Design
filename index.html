Welcome to Allam Marine

A British company with a longstanding manufacturing heritage

Allam Marine is an award-winning manufacturer and supplier of power generation machinery, components and control systems. Whether you’re a generating set manufacturer looking for engines with matching alternators or you require complete generator solutions, Allam Marine has the an unrivalled amount of knowledge, experience and stock, all under one roof.

We are renowned across the world for our ability and willingness to go the extra mile. When your market demands fast delivery at unbeatable prices, there's only one place in the world you can trust. Contact us today to find out how our experienced team can help power your business.
